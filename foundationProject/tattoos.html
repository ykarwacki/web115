Frequently Asked Questions

How old do I have to be?

You must be at least 18 years old and have a valid state ID. Sorry, but in the state of Illinois, your parent or guardian cannot give consent for us to tattoo if you are under age. Anyone under 18 in the shop must be accompanied by a parent or guardian and have the IDs to prove it.

Is it safe?

We follow a very strict sanitary procedure. All equipment and ink is fully sterilized and used only once. Because of this there is truly minimal risk of infection of any kind.

Can I bring my own design?

Please, bring us your design or even just your idea for a design. Tattoos are art, and like most artists, we don't want to do the same old thing all the time. We love custom work and can't wait to work with you on your tattoo no matter where you are in the design process.

How should I care for my tattoo?

Keep it clean and follow these procedures:

  1. Leave bandage on for 5-6 hours.
  2. Remove bandage, wash with warm water & soap, pat dry.
  3. Never bandage again.
  4. Before scab forms apply NEOSPORIN 3 or 4 times daily.
  5. Stay out of chlorine pools, salt water, and only minimum sun exposure while tattoo is healing.
  6. DON'T PICK AT SCAB. Tattoos will heal in 10-15 days.

Do tattoos hurt?

Yes. How much it hurts depends a great deal on where the tattoo is placed and what kind of work you have done. But ultimately, you either decide to confront the pain or not get tattooed.
