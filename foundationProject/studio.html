Our Studio

We want you to see our work environment before you come in, because we're proud of it. Cleanliness is probably the most important thing we do to ensure your safety. We take that very seriously

Our Sanitary Procedures

Every piece of equipment that will contact the skin is strictly single use. Before use, each piece is placed in a high-temperature autoclave for one full hour and then packeged in its own sterile wrapper which will be openned in front of you. All pieces are disposed of at the end of each session.

All ink is also individually sealed, openned only in the presence of the intended client, and any left is discarded. The tattoo area is carefully sanitized after each customer. All jewelry and piercing needles are full sterilized before each use.

Any reputable shop will take these procedures for granted and will be pleased to show you that is how they do things. In the event you decide to get work done elsewhere, we urge you to insist on no less.
