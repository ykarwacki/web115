Services We Offer

Refinishing

We can successfully complete just about any surface repair to wood furniture. Even in most cases of severe damage it is really just a matter of stripping the old finish and applying a new finish.

Our refinishing services also include veneer replacement and French polishing even on the most delicate antiques.

Furniture & Architectural Wood Repair

Sometimes an older piece of wood furnture or a wood architectural element is clearly missing one or more pieces.

We are well experienced and fully equiped for custom carving of replacement parts that will blend in with the restored orginal no matter how intricate or unusual the carving.

Cane & Rush Replacement

For dining room chairs made before the era of plastics, cane or rush weaving on seats and backs is common. It's also difficult to find someone who still knows how to replace it.

We can help. We are familar with all materials, practices, and weaving patterns that were typically used of these chairs.

Reupholstery

No need to take your freshly repaired wood frame to a separate upholsterer. As long as the furniture is primarily wood frame with upholstery added we can usually handle it in house.

We are not equiped to repair items fully covered in upholstery such as sofas.
