Advantages of All Grain Brewing

Using malt extract to brew great beer is fine, but after a time most dedicated home brewers feel the need to attempt brewing the way it was originally intended - with malted grain and quality water. If you're sitting on the fence about this, one thing you won't get from going all grain is a shorter easier brew. However, there are some advantages:

  • Brew results with a richer and more subtle flavor profile
  • Greater control over the size and character of recipies
  • Fully customized recipies
  • Maybe best of all, a deeper sense of mastery
